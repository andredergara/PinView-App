PinView is NOT a golf utility app.

PinView is now a vertical short-form social media platform for golf content.

The product direction should now feel much closer to:

* TikTok
* Instagram Reels
* YouTube Shorts

BUT entirely centered around golf culture and golf videos.

The current app foundation is getting there visually, but the overall experience still feels:

* clunky
* static
* disconnected
* not smooth enough
* not immersive enough

We are no longer building a concept app.
We are building a REAL social content platform.

DO NOT redesign the branding or aesthetic.
The black/green PinView golf-tech identity is strong.

Instead:
focus ENTIRELY on making the app feel:

* smooth
* addictive
* immersive
* alive
* social-media quality

---

## CORE PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY

The FEED is the product.

When users open PinView:
they should immediately enter a full-screen vertical golf content experience.

The app should emotionally feel like:
“Tiktok for golf.”

NOT:
“a golf utility app.”

---

1. REBUILD THE HOME FEED EXPERIENCE

---

The Home feed should now behave like a REAL short-form video platform.

Requirements:

* full-screen vertical videos
* one video per screen
* smooth vertical swipe transitions
* autoplay videos
* preload next videos
* immersive scrolling
* smooth gesture handling
* minimal loading interruptions

The experience should feel:

* fluid
* responsive
* premium
* addictive

similar to:

* TikTok
* Reels

---

2. PRIORITIZE VIDEO IMMERSION

---

Videos should dominate the screen.

Fix:

* awkward spacing
* empty UI gaps
* non-fullscreen layouts
* static card feeling

Videos should:

* properly crop vertically
* fill mobile displays
* autoplay smoothly
* loop seamlessly
* support mute/unmute
* maintain high quality rendering

---

3. POLISH SOCIAL INTERACTIONS

---

Likes, comments, follows, and saves should feel:

* instant
* animated
* satisfying

Requirements:

* heart turns red instantly
* subtle like animation
* comments slide smoothly
* follow button updates immediately
* save interactions feel responsive

NO delayed/clunky interactions.

Everything should feel native-mobile quality.

---

4. IMPROVE COMMENT EXPERIENCE

---

Comments should resemble modern social platforms.

Requirements:

* smooth opening animation
* bottom-sheet style comments
* profile pictures
* usernames
* timestamps like:

  * 5m
  * 2h
  * 1d
* smooth posting
* instant rendering

Commenting should feel:

* effortless
* fast
* polished

---

5. MAKE THE APP FEEL ALIVE

---

The platform should immediately feel active.

Ensure:

* seeded creators actually render
* videos load correctly
* discover page feels populated
* engagement counts display correctly
* profiles feel connected

No fake empty feeling.

---

6. IMPROVE DISCOVER PAGE

---

The Discover page should feel like:

* TikTok discover
* creator discovery
* trending golf content

Requirements:

* trending golfers
* trending clips
* search users
* smooth profile navigation
* responsive loading
* polished cards

Remove:

* awkward layouts
* broken follow logic
* self-follow options

---

7. MOBILE-FIRST PERFORMANCE

---

This app should feel built for iPhone first.

Optimize:

* swipe responsiveness
* touch interactions
* animation smoothness
* feed performance
* video buffering
* loading states

Everything should feel:

* native
* fluid
* modern

---

8. REMOVE “APP DEMO” FEELING

---

Right now parts of the app still feel:

* placeholder
* prototype-like
* disconnected

Eliminate:

* mock behavior
* broken transitions
* delayed interactions
* inconsistent rendering
* dead UI sections

The goal is:
users should forget this is an early-stage app.

---

9. IMPORTANT

---

DO NOT redesign the branding.
DO NOT change the PinView identity.

Keep:

* black/dark UI
* green accents
* premium golf-tech aesthetic
* cinematic golf atmosphere

ONLY improve:

* smoothness
* realism
* immersion
* responsiveness
* social-media quality UX

PinView should now begin feeling like:
“A real golf content platform people could genuinely get addicted to.”
